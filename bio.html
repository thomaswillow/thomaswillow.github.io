Thomas Willow is a Singer/Songwriter & Producer. His voice narrates the human condition and reflects upon the growing disconnect between us and the natural world. His sultry tone accompanied by cinematic production transcends the listener towards deep introspection.

Inspired by the songwriting and sonic atmosphere of Jeff Buckley, Bon Iver and Leonard Cohen, Thomas Willow’s sound sits in timeless space. Thomas Willow has amassed over 2 million streams and has gained the support of Radio X, Burberry, BBC Intro, DICE, KCRW, Amazing Radio, Line of Best Fit, With Guitars, London In Stereo.

Currently working on a new EP, Thomas Willow continues his journey of escapism.
