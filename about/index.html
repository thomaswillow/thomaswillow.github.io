Thomas Willow
Sunrise / Music / Merchandise / Bio

Thomas Willow is a Conductor, Pianist, Singer, Producer and Songwriter. Described by Clash magazine as a young Leonard Cohen, Thomas Willow’s music is an array of soft strings and piano melodies that underpin his soulful vocals.

Thomas Willow is the man behind “Willow & Friends choir“ that creates standalone events often in listed buildings or churches.

His music is timeless, elegant and profound. Qualities rare to come by in modern day music.

With over 2 million plays across digital streaming platforms, placements on New Music Friday & Easy Listening Spotify playlists, radio play on Radio X, BBC Introducing & Burberry Acoustic, he is incredibly dedicated to his craft.

Creating his own album artwork & music videos, Thomas Willow’s artistic vision can be seen from every angle of his creative pursuits.
